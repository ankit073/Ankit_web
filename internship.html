1. Solving Partial Differential Equations with finite Element Method in FEniCS


  • Guide: Dr. Gowrisankar S, Associte Professor, NIT Patna
  • Duration: 05 May 2025 to 04 July 2025
  • Institute: National Institute of Technology, Patna
  • View Project Report

    I completed a research internship at NIT Patna on the topic “Solving Partial Differential Equations with Finite Element Methods in FEniCS”. This project introduced me to the finite element method (FEM) for solving complex PDEs and gave me practical experience in numerical analysis, mathematical modeling, and scientific programming.
   Through this internship, I became familiar with the FEniCS computing platform, where I implemented FEM techniques to solve real mathematical problems. The experience not only deepened my understanding of applied mathematics but also strengthened my skills in problem-solving, coding, and research methodology.
